About Me

Hello! I'm an undergraduate Mathematics - Computer Science (Math-CS) student at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD), an intern at the San Diego Supercomputer Center (SDSC), and a tutor for the Computer Science Department at UCSD. Through my courses, internship, and tutoring, I developed my proficiency in Java, Python and HTML, with the capability to pick up new languages quickly.

I am also pursuing an Urban Planning minor at UCSD, with a focus on GIS, transportation planning, and public transportation. With GIS, I have gained an appreciation for data science, its role in urban planning to succinctly convey ideas, and the influence of data on our decision making.

With my proficiency in programming and growing knowledge of the tools and techniques of urban planning, I want to propel progress with equitable solutions, ensuring a future for the many, and not the few.
